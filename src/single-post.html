William Theodoracopulos 12.03.14

Australian designers, Kristina Sostarko and Jason Odd are the design duo behind the cool and graphic Inaluxe brand. The couple met at art school, and now share a home and studio in a small town, close to the Grampians National Park. Bold in style and colour, their prints combine a fresh hit of something modern, with a true retro flavour. Great graphic works for office or commercial spaces, equally brilliant for contemporary or vintage homes.
